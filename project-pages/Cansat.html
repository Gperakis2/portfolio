ATX – CANSAT 2015-2017
A team of Greek students from the European School Of Brussels III who participated in the Belgium CanSat Competition, a project that involves building a mini satellite inside a soda can.
Project Details
Client-server architecture: The system uses a client-server architecture, where one node (client) requests data from the other nodes (servers) and sends them to a gateway (Raspberry Pi) via serial communication.
Sensors and data processing: The system utilises different sensors on the Thunderboard Sense 2 platform, such as ambient light, inertial, and microphone sensors, to detect and count the number of people and vehicles entering or exiting the region. The microphone data is also fed to a neural network to classify the presence of people in the area.
Data visualization: The gateway receives and parses the data packets from the client and updates a remote database. A Flask server runs alongside the UART receiver and creates a data stream that displays the statistics on a website
